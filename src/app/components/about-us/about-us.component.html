This is a website where you can check for stock information.

Our website has 4 main functions:

1. Search for stock - you can search for a specific stock and get its information (name, ask/bid/open price, daily trading volume, P/E etc).

2. Morale booster - you can enter a stock name that you might have in your portfolio and you will be displayed a worse performing stock to boost your morale.

3. Compare stocks - you can search for two stocks and compare their stats side by side.

4. Market overview - you can get an overview of some of the most popular stocks on the front page.
